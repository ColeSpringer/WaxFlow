WaxFlow demo (dev mode)

Mints a signed URL via POST /sign and plays it. Direct play (format auto on an untransformed source) serves original bytes with full range support; anything else streams a live WAV transcode where the t= box seeks sample-exact.

HLS

Mints a signed /hls/master.m3u8 URL and plays it through hls.js (fetched from a CDN; this page is dev-mode only). Safari can also take the master URL natively.
